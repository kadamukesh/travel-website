Athirappilly and Vazhachal Waterfalls in Thrissur
As you walk down the stone slabs that lead to the base of the Athirappilly waterfalls, a mysterious serenity overcomes you. It is Kerala's most famous and largest waterfall at over 80 ft high. Getting there Nearest railway station: Chalakudy, about 30 km away Nearest airport: Cochin International Airport, about 53 km from Thrissur
